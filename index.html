GAME RULES:

  1. Click "Click Start" to play
  2. Select which version you would like to play. (Normal has static bubbles. Falling has bubbles that fall down.)
  3. When bubbles pop up, press the keys on your keyboard that correspond to letter in each bubble to remove that bubble.
  4. (Normal) An incorrect key press will deduct 200 points from your score. (Falling) An incorrect key press or skipping a bubble will result in a deduction of 50 points.
  5. Aim for the high score! Compete with people around the world.
  6. Have fun!

ABOUT THE GAME

Welcome to the page that will forever change the way you type. This website has a game (which you should totally check out, by the way) which is pretty cool and was actually made in about 4 or 5 days. (Amazing, isn't it?) To play the game, you'll have to click the tab on the side that says "Go To Game". But first, here's an intro to the game. The game is for those who want to improve their typing skills. It will feature bubbles that pop up on the screen that contain letters. You'll have to type that letter (or set of letters) to get a score. You get points for correct answers and deductions for incorrect typing. The best part about this is that you won't be able to look at your keyboard to have a high score. (Sorry, but that's the way you properly type.) After x amount of seconds, the game will end and your score will be totaled. High scores will be featured on the scoreboard.
